Photo Processing

When you apply AI effects in Dreamify, your selected photo is sent securely to our servers for temporary processing. As part of this process, the photo may be shared with trusted third-party AI providers, specifically Google Gemini API and Replicate API, solely for the purpose of generating the requested effects. The photo is used only momentarily during processing and is not stored or retained afterward. No photo data is used for training purposes or shared beyond the processing task.
